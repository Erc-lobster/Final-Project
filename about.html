    About Us

    LandLobster Pets was founded in 1874 with the goal of giving people a companion for life. We believe in giving LandLobsters new homes with loving families, and we strive to provide excellent customer service every step of the way.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible companions in the for our LandLobsters and excellent customer service. We are committed to exceeding our customers' expectations every step of the way.

    Meet The Team

    We are a dedicated team of professionals who are passionate about what we do. Our team consists of experienced LandLobster Wranglers, customer service professionals, and shipping and logistics experts. We work together to ensure that our customers are satisfied with their purchases and their overall experience with LandLobster Pets.

    CEO

    Our CEO is dedicated to running an ethical and supporting company that respects its employees and LandLobsters

    Head LandLobster Wrangler

    Our head wrangler has been working in the industry for over 15 years now, and is dedicated to safely catching LandLobsters and bring them to homes around the world.
